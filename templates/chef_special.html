Chef's Special

Meet Chef Subham

At the heart of Mirchi Bites' culinary magic is the masterful Chef Subham. With an impeccable palate and an unwavering passion for innovative cuisine, Chef Subham has carved a reputation as one of Odisha's finest chefs. His journey from an aspiring cook to a culinary visionary is nothing short of extraordinary.

Trained in traditional Indian cooking, Chef Subham elevates each dish with a modern twist, blending local flavors with global techniques. His creations are not just food; they are an art form—a true celebration of taste, texture, and color.

Whether it's a reinvented classic or an entirely new dish, every plate he serves carries his signature flair, making the dining experience at Mirchi Bites unforgettable.

Special Dishes by Chef Subham

Mutton Rogan Josh

One of Chef Subham's most celebrated dishes, the Mirchi Prawn Delight brings together the bold flavors of coastal Odisha with a fiery twist. Fresh, succulent prawns are marinated in a secret spice blend and grilled to perfection, served with a tangy tamarind sauce that will leave your taste buds craving more.

Chicken Bhuna Masala

This dish is a rich, indulgent treat that takes the humble chicken curry to new heights. Slow-cooked with fragrant spices, tomatoes, and onions, the Chicken Bhuna Masala is Chef Subham’s signature comfort food that packs a punch of flavor in every bite.

Hyderabadi Chicken Dum Biryani

Biryani Lovers , rejoice! Chef Subham’s Hyderabadi Biryani is here to win your hearts. This dish features perfectly charred cubes of chicken marinated in an array of spices, grilled to smoky perfection, layed with basmati rice and served with a refreshing mint curd that complements its bold flavors.
