Our Story

From Humble Beginnings

Mirchi Bites was born in the year 2003 in the heart of Cuttack, Odisha, out of the dreams and determination of two extraordinary individuals—Tusar Kanta Mohanty and Mitrajita Mohanty. What started as a modest hut-style dhaba serving authentic, homemade flavors has now grown into one of the most cherished food destinations in Odisha.

Back in 2003, the journey was anything but easy. With limited resources and immense passion, Tusar and Mitrajita opened their small eatery. Located by the roadside, their dhaba became a haven for food lovers seeking rich and authentic flavors at affordable prices. They poured their hearts into creating every dish, and slowly but surely, word spread about their food, dedication, and warm hospitality.

A Journey of Struggles and Success

The road to success was paved with challenges. Long days, sleepless nights, and the constant effort to stand out in a competitive food scene tested their resolve. But Tusar and Mitrajita never gave up. Their passion for food and their unwavering belief in creating a family-friendly space for all kept them going.

After years of hard work, the humble dhaba transformed into something much bigger. In 2024, Mirchi Bites now proudly operates more than 10 outlets across Odisha, serving a wide range of mouth-watering dishes that remain rooted in the flavors and love that started it all. Along the way, the brand has won several prestigious awards for culinary excellence, customer service, and innovation.

A Legacy of Taste

What sets Mirchi Bites apart is not just the food, but the story behind it. The success of this family-run business is a testament to the resilience, love, and shared vision of the couple. Tusar and Mitrajita continue to be involved in every aspect of the business, ensuring that the core values of quality, tradition, and warmth are present in every dish served.

From the small beginnings of a roadside dhaba to the bustling restaurants you see today, Mirchi Bites is more than just a place to eat—it's a symbol of dedication, dreams, and the beautiful journey of a couple who dared to dream big.
